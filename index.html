Biography

Huan-Chin earned her bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from National Taiwan University in 2017. During her studies, she conducted research on SDN (Software-defined networking) under the guidance of Prof. An-Yeu Wu, exploring the time complexity of the SDN database algorithm. Additionally, she worked on a project led by Prof. Yi-Chang Lu, focusing on HDRI (High Dynamic Range Imaging) to detect ghosting in a set of images with different exposures.


Driven by her passion for creative thinking, problem-solving, and curiosity, Huan-Chin pursued a career as a Product Manager. She joined Neousys Technology, a promising company in the IPC industry, where she honed her skills by collaborating with experienced professionals to develop sustainable and market-friendly products.


With a strong background in critical thinking and analysis, Huan-Chin excelled in product planning, evaluation, and development. She leveraged her expertise and relied on knowledge, facts, and data to make informed decisions, consistently achieving successful outcomes.


During her leisure time, Huan-Chin took the initiative to self-study web front-end and back-end design. Immersed in programming, she discovered a deep interest in utilizing code to solve problems, motivating her to pursue a career as a software engineer. Through relentless dedication, she completed over 30 projects, constantly challenging herself and nurturing her skills in the field.
